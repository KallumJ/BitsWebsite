We are a Semi-Vanilla server and stay true to the vanilla experience. However we use a minimal number of plugins to improve your quality of life and enjoyment while playing on the server. Information about each of them can be found below!

Simple Voice Chat

We make use of a mod called Simple Voice Chat, which adds proximity voice chat to Minecraft, so you can talk to other nearby players! It's mod page can be found here to get yourself a download. If you need help installing the mod, check out the tutorials page here.

Custom Plugin

Our custom plugin ensures allows us to add features we think are right for the server. It's highlight features are listed below:

  • /beam [player] - Requests to teleport to the provided player
  • /bed - Teleports the player back to the bed they last slept in
  • /rtp - Teleports the player to a random location on the map
  • /warp [warp] - Teleports the player to the specified warp. Only used for community areas, such as Spawn, Nether Hub, etc.
  • /nick [nickname] - Gives you the specified nickname
  • /challenges - Lots of challenges for cool rewards!
  • /freecam - Enters the player into freecam.
  • Allays can be bred with Golden Apples. If they are near eachother, they will breed, and go on a 5 minute cooldown, like normal animals!
  • Dragons have a 30% chance to drop an Elytra. No more endless end city searching!
  • Mobs can be silenced by naming then "Silence Me".
  • Item frames can be made invisible by splashing it with an invisibility potion.
  • Leaves decay instantly!
  • Mobs have a rare chance to drop their heads.
