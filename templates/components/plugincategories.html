We are a Semi-Vanilla server and stay true to the vanilla experience. However we use a minimal number of plugins to improve your quality of life and enjoyment while playing on the server. Information about each of them can be found below!

Custom Plugin

Our custom plugin ensures allows us to add features we think are right for the server. It's highlight features are listed below:

  • /beam [player] - Requests to teleport to the provided player at the cost of a Beam Token
  • /bed - Teleports the player back to the bed they last slept in
  • /rtp - Teleports the player to a random location on the map
  • /warp [warp] - Teleports the player to the specified warp. Only used for community areas, such as Spawn, Nether Hub, etc.
  • /nick [nickname] - Gives you the specified nickname

Custom Advancements

We add our own custom achievements called Bits Questing Mode that get updated every season that provide more to work towards and act as a nice guide through the game. The categories of advancements are:

Advancement Type
Farming/Food
Enchanting
Crafting
Weaponry/Combat
Resource Collection
The End
The Nether
Redstone

Some of the challenges found in the Questing mode will take some serious effort to complete, but they are all possible.

Custom Statistics

During your time playing the game, you may be rewarded for achieving a certain level of statistic. There are tons to discover, made up of both default Minecraft statistics and custom tracked ones, and each having 5 reward tiers of them. The more you work towards increasing the statistic, the better rewards you earn. Can you find them all, and top the leaderboards?
