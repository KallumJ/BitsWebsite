Rules and Whitelist

Interested in joining our community? We would love to have you! Give the rules below a read, and when your ready, head on over to our Discord server and submit your whitelist application in the #whitelist channel!

  • Rules

  • 1. Be respectful towards others, regardless of your liking towards them. Racism, harassment, sexism, etc is NOT tolerated.
  • 2. No advertising of any kind.
  • 3. No spamming of either short frequent messages, or extremely long copy pastas.
  • 4. No extreme NSFW content including your avatar, gifs, videos, nicknames, status, etc.
  • 5. No griefing or raiding

  • Whitelist Questions

  • 1. What is your username?
  • 2. How old are you?
  • 3. Why would you like to join the server?
  • 4. What is rule 3?
